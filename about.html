About me

I'm Devin Chanthirath, a man with many skills and hobbies. I am a Sunnyside Highschool and a 2 year CART graduate with a interest in graphic design, programming, and animation.

Speaking of CART, I started off in the MutiMedia labs as a graphic design student; additionally my team and I won the award for best presentation while I was the main graphic designer. This leads me to my final year as a Web Application Design student (know as web app) where I worked with companies such as EECU and the Fresno Chaffee Zoo to design apps intended for their profession. As this was my final year, I joined the Esports club originally as a fill-in teammate but quickly joined the main teams. Unfornately we didn't win the tournament but we fought and won against other teams who we thought were much stronger.

For my 5 years after graduating, I intend to go to Fresno State University to study computer science and transfer to UC Merced to farther deepen my studies and widen my view on the career pathways for computer science.
